The Story

The primary story of this universe is… very rough. Set to follow a human woman named Clementine, trying to escape the demon world with her life after the door between worlds is slammed shut. Focus is on her dynamic with the demon god, apples to oranges comparison.

There are also many smaller interpersonal stories to be told between demons.


What are demons?

Demons are a diverse group of humanoids, ranging from anthropomorphic animals to nearly indistinguishable from humans. Most lie somewhere in-between. They are made up of fruit, which replaces their internal organs; being sliced apart will reveal slices of their associated fruit and guts of a matching color. They feed on fruits grown from human souls.


How demons came to be

Once, the first demons were human worshippers of the Many-Faced God. In exchange for their devotion and their souls, they were planted in the soil and granted new life; the place that once housed their soul was replaced with fruit. However, this angered the Divine Amalgam, who created humans, so they fled to what is now called the demon realm (official name pending). Demons are typically born rather than made, now.


The Demon Realm

The demon realm is a smaller, parallel copy of the human realm. Small segments of the human realm are exactly copied and smashed together. The only truly unique part of the demon realm is at its very center - a massive garden called Eden. This is where the Many-Faced God resides (officially) and where human souls are planted. The souls then grow into fruiting plants and feed demonkind.


The Gods

This world has two gods - The Divine Amalgam and The Many-Faced One.

The Divine Amalgam

The Divine Amalgam is broadly THE creator deity. It is a raw, beastial creature constructed of exposed flesh. It is NOT happy about the Many-Faced defiling and stealing from its creation.

The Many-Faced One

The Many-Faced One is a shapeshifting trickster and creator of demons. Much like the Divine Amalgam, its natural form is a massive beast. However, it also likes to take on humanoid form; it created an identity for itself as an apple fruit bat named Jazz. However, it is also prone to borrowing the appearance of an existing demon. In the demon realm, it’s said that encountering your doppelganger is a personal encounter with god.

Most demons believe that their face is a gift from god; a mask from their collection they have been given to wear for the duration of their life; and, after they die, perhaps someone else will wear it next.


The Festival of Faces

Demons have an annual festival in honor of their god, called the Festival of Faces. In order to celebrate both its shapeshifting abilities and the gift of their individual faces, they wear masks of their own face. Craftsmanship varies wildly, from masks that are literally indistinguishable from a real face to a child’s paper and glue craft. Throughout the festival, demons will trade masks with one another, ultimately aiming to end the night with a face they’ve never seen before.


Characters

Ana & Sherry

Two of the first demons I designed, and in need of a rework. These two are a pineapple tiger shark & strawberry nurse shark mother and daughter. They also have a third family member, a little lemon shark boy.


Merry, Wasp, Fēn

Merry is a merlot grape sheep; Ms Wasp, who has yet to be officially named, is a fig wasp. Fēn is a snake pear - specifically a pearl-banded rat snake. Her design is not final and needs to be revisited.


Morrigan, Nila, Sienna, Scarlett, Alice


Morrigan is a crowberry crow; she has a sibling (yet to be designed) who is a gooseberry goose. Nila is a blueberry black bear with pie theming; Sienna is a cloudberry polar bear with pancakes and jam theming. They run Three Beary Bakery together with a lingonberry grizzy (also yet to be designed.) Scarlett is a pitaya dragon who likes to skateboard; she is best friends with Alice, a strawberry bunny.


Margot, Darla, Centipede

Margot is a lime mouse; she used to be a bartender but lost the position because she spent too much time getting drunk instead of working. She is now a DJ. Darla is a durian skunk and a big smoker; she always smells like weed. The two are an inseperable pair of best friends and party girls. They also have a (to be designed) friend named Ange, an orange dog. Ms Pomegranite Centipede, who is yet to be named, owns and manages the club they frequent and work at.



Mara

Mara Ann Schino is a cherry fox. She is an explosive inventor with a big personality.


Calla, Miriam

The first two demons I ever designed, and the start of this universe! Calla is a lemon demon & an incredibly talented mask-maker. She has dozens of masks that are perfect replicas of her own face. Miriam is a far more sociable and outgoing demon. She's a huge gossip and will ruin your life for fun.
